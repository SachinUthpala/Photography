Welcome to M L Photography

Growing up amidst Scotland’s breathtaking scenery, Malcolm developed an eye for nature’s intricate details. His work showcases the splendor of the Scottish wilderness, from majestic mountains to serene lochs. While his heart lies in landscape and wildlife photography, Malcolm’s versatility extends to weddings, portraits, and special events. He blends natural beauty with human emotion, ensuring every photograph tells a unique story.
